## ğŸ¯ **Image Guidelines**

### **Style Requirements:**
- **Color Palette**: Match the site's colors (#2b2d42, #8d99ae, #edf2f4, #ef233c)
- **Mood**: Gritty, confident, vintage Americana
- **Lighting**: Warm, dramatic, or natural lighting

- **Atmospheric**: Capture the barbershop vibe and culture
